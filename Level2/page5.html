Imagine yourself in a masterchef competition.
You are in a team challenge and cooking a chicken dish. Every member has been assigned a task.

Member A will wash the chicken
Member B will marinate the washed chicken
Member C will cook the marinated chicken.
You notice how Member B’s work is dependent on Member A’s output and Member C’s work is dependent on Member B’s output. If member A doesn’t wash the chicken and give to Member B, member B will have nothing to marinate.
