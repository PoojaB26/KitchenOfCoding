Now lets get back to boiling.
We need water for boiling
If I’m making tea for 2 people,
I will be needing 2 cups of water.

But when I’m making rice for 4 people,
I might need 4 glasses of water

Or when I’m making chutney for 2 people,
I will need 1 teaspoon of water

So one dish may require 2 cups of water, another dish may require 5. You see, how the quantity could vary?
In coding language, we can say water is a Variable. It varies for dish to dish.
