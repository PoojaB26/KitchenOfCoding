Arithmetic operations.

We can have all kinds of operations on variables. We are adding tea_powder and sugar to boiled_water to make raw_tea. We added something to boiled_water. But the sugar we added to boiled_water must be subtracted from our sugar container, so now our sugar container has 2 teaspoons of sugar less.

Let’s take this simple problem.

If the sugar container had 10 teaspoons of sugar, and we took 2 teaspoons, then how many teaspoons of sugar is left?
